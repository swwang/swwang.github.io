Our Story

  • First Encounter

    December 4, 2016

    Sharon & Brian meet for the very first time at Koja Kitchen. They hit it off immediately when Sharon buys Brian some Kamikaze Fries even though she forgot his name. Afterwards, they sit together on the roller coasters at Great America which gets their hearts racing - was it the other person or the rides?

  • Getting the Digits

    March 26, 2017

    Sharon comes over to Brian’s place for a Sherlock viewing party. They download Allo, Google’s latest chat app and spend 2 hours sending stickers to each other after everyone else has left.

  • Into You

    June 24, 2017

    Sharon invites Brian to be her +1 at the ID10T music festival. At the end of the night, Brian drives Sharon home as she sings along to “Into You” by Arianna Grande. Brian is too nervous about his plans to ask Sharon out and misses the hint.

  • Quid Pro Quo

    June 28, 2017

    Brian visits Sharon at her secret workplace and eats free sushi. In return, Brian invites Sharon to his apartment where she helps him pack all night for his move the very next day.

  • First Date

    July 7, 2017

    Brian takes Sharon out to Lotus Thai Bistro for their very first date. It is a great success.

  • The Big Puzzle

    Feb 18, 2024

    Brian proposes to Sharon after she completes a custom-made puzzle hunt.
