    Travel 交通住宿
    travel Flying In 飞行 We highly recommend flying to SJC (San Jose) if possible. SFO (San Francisco) and OAK (Oakland) are also doable, but quite a bit further. 我们建议飞往圣何塞 (SJC)。你也可以飞往旧金山 (SFO) 或奥克兰 (OAK), 但距离都比较远。
    airport_shuttle Transportation 交通 We highly recommend renting a car while you're in the Bay Area. Public transportation is difficult to use near and around our venue in Santa Cruz. Highway 17 is the main route to Santa Cruz and can be hilly and windy. 我们建议租一辆车。山区和海滩的公共交通不太好。通往海滩的道路穿过山区, 可能会有车辆通行。
    hotel Accommodation 酒店 We recommend staying near the venue around Scotts Valley or Santa Cruz. The main route in and out of Santa Cruz (Highway 17) is windy and can be congested due to beach traffic. We have a room block at Sheraton Four Points with discounted rates. 我们建议住在婚礼场地附近。为了您的方便, 我们在Sheraton Four Points 预订了一些房间。

    FAQs 常问问题
    checkroom What should I wear? 着装要求是什么？ Cocktail attire! Our venue is tucked away in a redwood forest, so please bring the appropriate footwear for walking on grass and dirt paths when moving between event spaces. We also recommend bringing a light jacket as it can get chilly in the evening. 鸡尾酒会服装！我们的场地藏匿在红杉林中, 因此在活动场地之间移动时, 请带上合适的鞋子, 准备好在草地和泥土小径上行走。晚上可能有点凉, 我们也建议你带一件薄外套。
    garage Is there parking? 有停车位吗？ There is limited parking (~40 spots) at the venue so we highly encourage carpooling. Reach out if you would like us to help pair you up! 我们的场地共有40个停车位。请与他人拼车。如果您希望我们帮助您配对, 请跟我们联系！
    accessibility Is the venue accessible? 场地无障碍吗？ The venue's walking paths are dirt trails that can be sloped and uneven. For those who wish to minimize walking, we have VIP parking as well as a golf cart for getting around. 场地的步行道是土路，可能有坡度且不平坦。如果希望尽量减少步行，我们提供贵宾停车位和高尔夫球车供您出行。
    apparel What else should I pack? 这次旅行我还应该带些什么？ California has beautiful weather with warm sunny days, but can be a bit chilly at night. We recommend bringing sunscreen for the day and layers or a light jacket for nights. Sunglasses are highly recommended for driving. 加州的天气很好, 但你应该带上防晒霜和夹克。开车时太阳镜很有用。
    featured_seasonal_and_gifts Is there a gift registry? 有礼品登记吗？ No :). Your presence is the true gift. 没有:)。我们只是想见到大家！
    email I have a specific question! 我有不同的问题！ No problem. Please send us an email at sbteam33 AT gmail.com 没问题。请发电子邮件至： sbteam33 AT gmail.com
