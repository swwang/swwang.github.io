Travel 交通住宿
travel Flying In We highly recommend flying to SJC (San Jose) if possible. SFO (San Francisco) and OAK (Oakland) are also doable, but quite a bit further.
airport_shuttle Transportation We highly recommend renting a car while you're in the Bay Area. Public transportation is difficult to use near and around our venue in Santa Cruz. Highway 17 is the main route to Santa Cruz and can be hilly and windy.
hotel Accomodation We recommend staying near the venue around Scotts Valley or Santa Cruz. The main route in and out of Santa Cruz (Highway 17) is windy and can be congested due to beach traffic. We have a room block at Sheraton Four Points with discounted rates--just make sure to mention our names when you reach out.
